1. Overview

Donelist-Build Your Motivation ("the App") is a productivity tool that helps you track daily tasks and build motivation. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Task Data (Stored Locally & iCloud Sync)

  • Task titles and content you create
  • Task completion status
  • Task creation and completion dates
  • Repeating task settings

Storage: All task data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. When you are signed in to iCloud, your task data is automatically synced to your personal iCloud account for backup and cross-device access. We do not have access to your task content - all data remains in your personal iCloud account.

2.2 App Settings

  • Animation preferences (on/off)
  • Sparkle effect preferences (on/off)

Storage: Stored locally on your device.

2.3 Installation Timestamp

  • First app launch date (for feature eligibility)

Storage: Stored in iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync across your Apple devices.

Important: Your task data is automatically synced to your iCloud account when you are signed in to iCloud. This sync is automatic and requires no action from you. If you are not signed in to iCloud, your data remains local only.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ Photos or media files
  • ❌ Device identifiers for tracking
  • ❌ Usage analytics or behavioral data

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Task Management: Display and manage your tasks
  • Statistics: Calculate completion rates and streaks (locally)
  • Feature Eligibility: Determine access to features based on installation date

All processing happens locally on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Your task data never leaves your device except:

  • When you explicitly export data using the Export feature
  • When automatically synced to your personal iCloud account (handled by Apple's CloudKit)
  • Installation timestamp synced via iCloud Key-Value Storage (Apple's service)

Important: iCloud sync is between your devices only, using your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this synced data.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Apple Services

  • iCloud CloudKit: Automatically syncs your task data across your devices when you're signed in to iCloud
  • iCloud Key-Value Storage: Syncs installation timestamp for feature eligibility
  • App Store: Standard distribution and updates

Note: All iCloud sync is handled by Apple's infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

6.2 No Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any third-party tracking services.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the App
  • Edit: Modify or delete tasks anytime
  • Export: Export your data via Settings → Export Data
  • Delete: Delete the App to remove all local data

8. Data Security

  • Data stored locally using Apple's Core Data framework
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit with end-to-end encryption
  • No data transmitted to our servers or third-party servers
  • You are responsible for device security (passcode, Face ID, etc.) and iCloud account security

9. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use means you accept the changes.
